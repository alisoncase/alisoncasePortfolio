Ukrainian Refugees in Europe

April 2024

This map explores how European countries are hosting Ukrainian refugees in varied proportions and poses questions for the future based on the shifting political landscapes. I developed the map in ArcGIS Pro and Adobe Illustrator. It served as the final project for my Introduction to Cartography course at the University of Wisconsin–Madison.
